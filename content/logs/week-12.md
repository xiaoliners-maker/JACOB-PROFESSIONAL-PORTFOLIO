## Overview

Kicked off a full week dedicated to building a **professional portfolio** from scratch using **Next.js** and **Tailwind CSS**. The week progressed from planning and scaffolding to feature development, content additions, and final preparations for deployment on Vercel.

## Day-by-Day

**May 4** — Planned the development of a professional portfolio using **Next.js** and **Tailwind CSS**, outlining core features and overall structure.

**May 5** — Initiated development, focusing on establishing the **project structure** and implementing core features of the portfolio.

**May 6** — Continued portfolio development with plans to incorporate a **weekly logs section** to document activities throughout the internship.

**May 7** — Added a new **project card** to the portfolio to better highlight completed work and improve the overall presentation.

**May 9** — Applied final refinements to the portfolio and prepared it for **deployment on Vercel**.

## Key Learnings

- Scaffolding and structuring a Next.js project from the ground up
- Building responsive UI components with Tailwind CSS utility classes
- Iterating on portfolio content and layout across a focused development sprint
- Preparing a Next.js application for production deployment on Vercel

## Challenges

Balancing feature completeness with polish within a single week — deciding which sections (project cards, weekly logs) were priority and ensuring the site was presentable before deployment, rather than adding scope indefinitely.
