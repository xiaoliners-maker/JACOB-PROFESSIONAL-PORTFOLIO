A week of technical learning and the exciting kickoff of a new hackathon project. Studied Node.js more deeply, attended the Badger Mint Seminar, conducted group research on digital marketing concepts, and started building for **Hack4Mapandan**.

**Feb 27** — Continued and further developed the Hack4Mapandan project system. Developed a **landing page** for the project.
